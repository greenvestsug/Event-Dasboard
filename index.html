Risks and Safety

  • Always wear gloves: Wear gloves when picking rubbish to protect your hands
  • Don't compress rubbish: Avoid using your hands or feet to compress rubbish
  • Be traffic aware: Pause litter picking to allow vehicles and motorcycles to pass safely
  • Lift safely: Don't overfill bags or lift heavy sacks alone
  • Clean hands: Wash hands before eating or drinking
  • Dress properly: Wear sturdy shoes and weather appropriate clothing
  • Avoid hazardous areas: Stay clear of water, rocks or other unknown objects
